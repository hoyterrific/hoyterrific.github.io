About: Associative Press is a game about the inherent bias in news. In the game, you play
as a cameraman for a news network. Each day, you are sent to the scene of a wartime
event, be it a rally or execution. Depending on what is filmed, the bias of the
country will shift either towards the Loyalists,who want to keep the current president
in power, or the Rebels, who are fighting for change in government.
